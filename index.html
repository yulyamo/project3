Best Book

My favorite book is "The Power Of Now" by Eckhart Tolle. The book is about becoming enlightened and finding peace and joy by tapping into your consciousness and by living in the moment.

The core message in the book is that our conditioned mind, conventional thinking pattern and sense of self prevents us from seeing the world and ourselves unfiltered. All true relationships are blocked by our beliefs created by incessant mental noise that have no objective reality. Once we learn the art of freeing ourselves from our thoughts, observing our thoughts, good and bad without judgment and being fully present in the moment will we be able to tap into our consciousness which with constant practise will lead to enlightenment.
